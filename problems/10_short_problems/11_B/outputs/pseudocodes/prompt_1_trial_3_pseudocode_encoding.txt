**Pseudocode: Finding Integer with Specific Property**

1. **Initialize** the variable `n` to the absolute value of the integer input from the user.
2. **Initialize** the counter `i` to 0.
3. **Repeat indefinitely** (loop):
   - Calculate the sum `s` which is the sum of the first `i` natural numbers (use the formula: s = i * (i + 1) / 2).
   - Calculate the difference `m` as `s - n`.
   - **If** the sum `s` is equal to `n`:
     - Output the value of `i`.
     - **Exit** the loop.
   - **Else if** the sum `s` is greater than `n`:
     - **If** the difference `m` is even (i.e., `m % 2` equals 0):
       - Output the value of `i`.
       - **Exit** the loop.
   - **Increment** the counter `i` by 1. 

**End of Pseudocode**. 

**Input**: An integer from the user.
**Output**: An integer `i` that meets the specified conditions.
